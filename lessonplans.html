Sonatina

  • Back-to-back practice m. 3 and m.6
  • Back-to-back practice m. 11-12 and m. 15-16
  • Have whole piece memorized and ready to play from memory by next week:
    • Thursday - play each * section from memory, work on back-to-back practice
    • Friday - your goal for today: play each * section one at a time without mistake
    • Saturday/Sunday - play two * sections per day (play it well 3x)
    • Monday - play WHOLE piece from memory, just try!
    • Tuesday - play WHOLE piece from memory, if there are any trouble measures, write them down in a list so we can work on them next lesson

Trumpet and the Echo

  • First, correct the rhythm on m.5-6, 10-12 think “long note, long note”
  • Make a copy of the music, cut it up into jigsaw pieces, see if you can put it back together
    • this is a good way to review if you know the piece!
  • I will ask you to play the whole piece from memory next week!

Sample Advanced Lesson Plan

Most annotations are written directly into these student’s scores. Students are encouraged to record their lessons on a device to listen to later.

Kuhlau:

  • Nit-picky details :), attend to these first before running through larger sections!
  • Take time getting from A section to B section - don’t rush
  • m.8 (and all others like it) make it an up motion, then take time afterward (like a period, then an indent; not the other way around)
  • Take time getting from A section to C section
  • Practice A section sixteenth notes for clarity. Your RH is messing your LH up!
    • Impulse practice, both hands together, stop on arrival points
    • Back-to-back practice all sixteenth note runs
    • Put in context

Telemann

  • Quiz yourself: with the music, could you start at any random measure without stumbling?
  • All tricky finger sections - play light and softly to avoid tension
  • M.22-23 don’t rush the eighth notes
  • m.48 start softer
  • M.43 - wait longer at the rest, enjoy the silences

Schumann (Fantasy Dance)

  • Start learning notes by playing one voice at a time/li>
  • In the LH B sections, write fingering
  • In the RH sections, learn by blocking chords first
