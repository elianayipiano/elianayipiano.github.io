About Me

I’m a pianist, accompanist, church musician, cellist, soloist, teacher, cartoonist (in my spare time) and am pursuing a B.M. in Piano Performance as SMU's Meadows School of the Arts. I have 3 years of experience in the teaching world and over 12 years studying the Piano.
